Todays news
One of the latest headlines is about Pope Francis announcing a Consistory for the creation of 21 new Cardinals, which will take place on December 7, 2024, in the Vatican. The new Cardinals come from a variety of regions worldwide, reflecting the universal nature of the Church. This event is significant as it precedes the opening of the 2025 Jubilee of Hope and follows the completion of the Second Session of the Synod on Synodality. The announcement highlights the Pope's continued effort to strengthen global connections within the Church.
